Meet Your Travel Designer

Carly Davis

Some people stumble into their calling. I booked my first international trip, fell completely in love with the process of discovery, and never looked back.

Six years in the travel industry have only deepened that passion. Today I work with clients who want more than a vacation. They want an experience that feels effortless, intentional, and entirely their own.

Having traveled extensively myself, I bring firsthand knowledge and genuine passion to every itinerary I design. Whether you're dreaming of an iconic North American adventure, from a Disney family milestone and Caribbean getaway to the mountains of Colorado or the coastlines of California, a romantic escape in Paris, or a once-in-a-lifetime journey through Australia and New Zealand — I build each experience around you. Your pace, your preferences, your vision.

My job isn't just to book your trip. It's to make sure every detail is thought of before you even think to ask — whether that's a hotel stay, a fully crafted itinerary, or a cruise. You simply arrive.
